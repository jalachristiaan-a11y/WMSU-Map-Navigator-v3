Who We Are

About Us

We are a team of WMSU innovators—proud students who believe that our university deserves a digital experience as grand as its physical history. The Crimson Navigator was born out of a desire to help our fellow Normalites, visitors, and guests find their way through our sprawling campus with confidence and ease.


We’ve been building this for 3 weeks, fueled by coffee and university pride. Though we only started our web development journey 2 months ago, we are constantly learning and iterating to make this the best possible tool for our community.


This project is for the WMSU family. We are committed to making navigation effortless, and we’d love to hear your ideas on how to make it even better.
