Campus Safety Protocol

×

1. Purpose of Use

The WMSU Map Navigator is provided solely for the purpose of legitimate navigation and exploration of the Western Mindanao State University campus. It is intended to assist students, faculty, staff, and visitors in locating buildings, facilities, and points of interest within the university grounds.

2. Prohibited Activities

Users are strictly prohibited from using this map system for any unlawful, unauthorized, or malicious purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Planning or facilitating illegal activities, including acts of terrorism, vandalism, or any form of criminal behavior.
  • Unauthorized data scraping, collection, or distribution of sensitive information about campus infrastructure or personnel.
  • Attempting to gain unauthorized access to university systems or restricted areas.
  • Harassment, intimidation, or any actions that threaten the safety and security of individuals or property on campus.
  • Disrupting the normal operations of the university or its digital services.

3. Data Privacy

By using this service, you agree to the collection and processing of your registration data (name, email, role, and for visitors, purpose of access and ID photo) for account management, security monitoring, and to ensure compliance with university policies. Your data will be handled in accordance with WMSU's privacy policy and applicable data protection laws.

4. Consequences of Misuse

Any detected misuse or violation of these protocols will result in immediate account termination. Furthermore, WMSU reserves the right to report any suspicious or malicious activities to campus security, local law enforcement agencies, and other relevant authorities. Users found to be in violation may face legal prosecution and/or disciplinary action by the university.

5. Agreement

By checking the "I agree to the Campus Safety Protocol" box and creating an account, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to abide by these terms and conditions. Your continued use of the WMSU Map Navigator signifies your acceptance of these protocols.
