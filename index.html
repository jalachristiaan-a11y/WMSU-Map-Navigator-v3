How to Use WMSU Map Navigator

1

Log in to your account to access the interactive campus map.

2

Choose which campus you want to explore — either Campus A or Campus B.

3

Select a building type from the list or use the search bar to find a specific building.

4

Click on the building to view images, descriptions, and navigation options.

5

Select “Show Path” to display the route directly on the map and start navigating.
